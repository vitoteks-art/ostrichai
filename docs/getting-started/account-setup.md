# Account Setup Guide

This guide will help you set up and optimize your account for the best experience with our AI-powered content creation platform.

## Creating Your Account

### Registration Options

You can create an account using:

1. **Email & Password**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Verify your email address

2. **Google Account**
   - One-click authentication
   - Automatic profile creation
   - Secure and convenient

3. **Social Login** (if available)
   - Quick access with existing accounts

### Email Verification

After registration:
1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account will be activated instantly

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Click "Resend verification" in the app
- Contact support if issues persist

## Setting Up Your Profile

### Basic Information

1. **Profile Picture**
   - Upload a professional headshot
   - Use a square image (recommended: 400x400px)
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG

2. **Personal Details**
   - Full name (for personalized experience)
   - Business name (if applicable)
   - Industry/category
   - Location (helps with localized content)

3. **Contact Information**
   - Phone number (for account security)
   - Alternative email (for recovery)

### Business Information (Optional)

For business users:
- **Company Name**
- **Industry** (Technology, Healthcare, Education, etc.)
- **Company Size** (Startup, SME, Enterprise)
- **Role** (Owner, Marketing Manager, Designer, etc.)

## Account Security

### Password Security

- Use a unique password for this platform
- Minimum 8 characters with mixed case
- Include numbers and special characters
- Change password regularly

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enable 2FA for enhanced security:

1. Go to **Profile → Security**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Choose your method:
   - **SMS** - Code sent to your phone
   - **Authenticator App** - More secure option
4. Save backup codes in a safe place

### Login Sessions

- View active sessions in **Profile → Security**
- Log out of sessions on other devices
- Monitor login activity for suspicious access

## Troubleshooting Account Issues

### Login Problems

**Forgot Password:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the reset instructions
4. Create a new secure password

**Account Locked:**
- Contact support with your email
- Provide verification details
- Account will be unlocked within 24 hours

**Suspicious Activity:**
- Change your password immediately
- Enable 2FA if not already active
- Review recent login activity

### Profile Issues

**Can't Update Profile:**
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if persists

**Upload Failures:**
- Check file size limits (max 5MB for images)
- Ensure supported format (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Try compressing the file
