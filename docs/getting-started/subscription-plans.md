## How to Upgrade

### From Starter to Professional

1. **Go to Subscription Page**
   - Click "Subscription" in navigation
   - Or click "Upgrade" buttons throughout the app

2. **Choose Your Plan**
   - Select "Professional Plan"
   - Choose billing cycle (monthly recommended)

3. **Payment Method**
   - Select Nigerian payment method
   - Enter payment details
   - Complete verification

4. **Confirmation**
   - Receive email confirmation
   - Access premium features immediately
   - Download invoice for records

### From Professional to Enterprise

1. **Contact Sales**
   - Click "Contact Sales" on subscription page
   - Schedule a consultation call

2. **Custom Quote**
   - Discuss your specific needs
   - Receive customized pricing

3. **Onboarding**
   - Dedicated setup session
   - Team training if needed
   - Custom configuration

## Managing Your Subscription

### Billing Information

**Update Payment Method:**
- Go to **Profile → Billing**
- Add new payment method
- Set as default for future payments

**Download Invoices:**
- **Profile → Billing → Invoices**
- Download PDF receipts
- Access payment history

**Billing Address:**
- Update for tax compliance
- Change for invoice delivery

### Plan Changes

**Upgrade Plan:**
- Instant upgrade available
- Prorated billing for mid-cycle changes
- Immediate access to new features

**Downgrade Plan:**
- Takes effect next billing cycle
- May lose access to some features
- Data retained for 30 days

**Cancel Subscription:**
- Access through **Profile → Billing**
- Data retained for 30 days
- Reactivate anytime

Yes! Upgrade instantly, downgrade at next billing cycle.

Yes! Save 20% with annual billing on Professional and Enterprise plans.

Yes, you can have multiple cards/bank accounts saved.
