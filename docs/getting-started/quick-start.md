# 🚀 Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your AI-powered content creation platform! This guide will get you up and running in just 5 minutes.

## 1. Create Your Account (1 minute)

1. **Visit the platform** - Go to your application URL
2. **Sign up** - Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. **Choose your method**:
   - **Email & Password** - Quick and easy
   - **Google Account** - One-click authentication
4. **Verify your email** (if required)

**That's it!** You now have access to the free Starter plan.

## 2. Explore Your Dashboard (2 minutes)

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard:

- **📊 Overview Stats** - See your project counts and usage
- **⚡ Quick Actions** - Fast access to create videos, logos, ads, and flyers
- **📋 Recent Projects** - Your latest work at a glance
- **🔔 Notifications** - Important updates and tips

**Pro tip:** Take a moment to explore each section to familiarize yourself with the interface.

## 3. Create Your First Project (2 minutes)

Let's create something amazing! Try one of these:

### 🎬 Create a Video (Recommended for beginners)
1. Click **"Create Video"** from Quick Actions
2. Enter your **product/service name**
3. Describe what you want the video to show
4. Choose a **style/template** (if available)
5. Click **"Generate Video"**

### 🎨 Design a Logo
1. Click **"Design Logo"** from Quick Actions
2. Enter your **business name**
3. Describe your **industry/style preferences**
4. Choose **color preferences**
5. Click **"Generate Logo"**

### 📱 Make Ad Creative
1. Click **"Make Ad Creative"** from Quick Actions
2. Upload or describe your **product**
3. Enter your **key message**
4. Select **target audience**
5. Click **"Generate Ad"**

## 4. Manage Your Projects

- **View all projects** - Click "Projects" in the navigation
- **Track progress** - See real-time generation status
- **Download results** - Get your finished creations
- **Share projects** - Collaborate with team members

## 5. Upgrade (When Ready)

The free Starter plan gives you:
- ✅ 5 videos per month
- ✅ 3 logos per month
- ✅ 5 ads per month
- ✅ 100MB storage

When you need more:
1. Go to **"Subscription"** in the navigation
2. Choose **"Professional Plan"** (₦2,900/month)
3. Complete payment with **Nigerian payment methods**
4. Enjoy unlimited features!
