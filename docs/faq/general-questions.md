## 🚀 Getting Started

### How do I create my first project?
1. **Sign up** for a free account
2. **Choose a tool** from your dashboard (Video, Logo, Ad Creative, etc.)
3. **Describe your project** in detail
4. **Customize** the results to match your vision
5. **Export** your finished creation

### What's included in the free plan?
- ✅ 5 videos per month
- ✅ 3 logos per month
- ✅ 5 ad creatives per month
- ✅ 100MB storage
- ✅ Basic templates and styles
- ✅ Standard support

### How do I upgrade my plan?
1. Go to **"Subscription"** in the main navigation
2. Choose **"Professional Plan"** (₦2,900/month)
3. Select your payment method (cards, bank transfer, mobile money)
4. Complete payment and enjoy premium features immediately!
