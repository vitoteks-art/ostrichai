### Why is my payment failing?
**Common Payment Issues:**

**Card Payments:**
- ❌ **Insufficient funds** - Check your account balance
- ❌ **Wrong details** - Verify card number, expiry, CVV
- ❌ **Card blocked** - Contact your bank
- ❌ **International limit** - Some banks block international payments

**Bank Transfers:**
- ❌ **Wrong account details** - Double-check beneficiary information
- ❌ **Transfer limit** - Exceed daily/instant transfer limits
- ❌ **Bank maintenance** - Try again later

**Mobile Money:**
- ❌ **Insufficient airtime** - Top up your mobile money account
- ❌ **Network issues** - Try different mobile network
- ❌ **Wrong PIN** - Verify your mobile money PIN

## 🚨 Common Billing Issues

### "Payment Failed" Error
**Troubleshooting Steps:**
1. **Check Account Balance** - Ensure sufficient funds
2. **Verify Card Details** - Double-check number, expiry, CVV
3. **Contact Bank** - Some banks block online payments
4. **Try Different Method** - Use alternative payment option
5. **Wait and Retry** - Temporary network issues

### "Subscription Not Activated"
**Activation Issues:**
1. **Check Email** - Confirmation email may be in spam
2. **Refresh Dashboard** - Pull down to refresh
3. **Log Out/In** - Sometimes fixes activation issues
4. **Contact Support** - We'll activate manually if needed

### "Double Charged" Issue
**Duplicate Charges:**
1. **Check Bank Statement** - Verify actual charges
2. **Pending vs Posted** - Some charges show as pending
3. **Multiple Attempts** - Failed payments may show temporarily
4. **Contact Support** - We'll investigate and refund if needed
