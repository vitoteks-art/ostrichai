## 📱 Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Facebook & Instagram Ads
- **Image Ads** - 1200x628px (1.91:1 ratio)
- **Video Ads** - Up to 240 minutes, 4GB max
- **Carousel Ads** - 2-10 images/videos
- **Stories Ads** - 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)

### Google Display Ads
- **Banner Ads** - Multiple standard sizes
- **Responsive Ads** - Auto-resize for all placements
- **Native Ads** - Blend with website content
- **Gmail Ads** - Email-integrated advertisements

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Sponsored Content** - Native feed advertisements
- **Message Ads** - Direct messaging campaigns
- **Dynamic Ads** - Personalized content
- **Text Ads** - Simple text-based promotions

## 💡 Copywriting Best Practices

### Headlines
- **Benefit-Focused** - "Get Results" vs "Our Product"
- **Urgency** - Limited time offers, scarcity
- **Curiosity** - Pose questions, teasers
- **Specificity** - Concrete numbers and details

### Body Copy
- **Problem-Solution** - Identify pain, offer solution
- **Social Proof** - Testimonials, reviews, stats
- **Risk Reversal** - Guarantees, free trials
- **Value Proposition** - Clear benefits and outcomes

### Call-to-Action (CTA)
- **Action-Oriented** - "Buy Now", "Learn More", "Get Started"
- **Benefit-Driven** - "Save Time", "Make Money", "Lose Weight"
- **Urgency-Creating** - "Limited Time", "Today Only"
- **Low-Commitment** - "Free Trial", "Download Now"

## 📊 Analytics & Measurement

### Key Metrics
- **Impressions** - How many people saw your ad
- **Clicks** - How many people clicked through
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** - Clicks divided by impressions
- **Conversions** - Desired actions completed
- **Cost Per Click (CPC)** - Average cost per click
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** - Revenue vs ad spend

### Attribution Tracking
- **View-Through Conversions** - Conversions after ad view
- **Click-Through Conversions** - Conversions after ad click
- **Multi-Touch Attribution** - Credit across multiple touchpoints
- **Conversion Window** - Time frame for attribution

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Low Click-Through Rate**
- Improve creative quality and relevance
- Test different headlines and images
- Refine targeting parameters
- Check ad placement performance

**High Cost Per Click**
- Improve quality score with better relevance
- Refine targeting to reach better audience
- Test different bidding strategies
- Improve landing page experience

**Poor Conversion Rate**
- Check landing page relevance and loading speed
- Simplify conversion process
- Add trust signals and social proof
- Test different offers and incentives

**Ad Disapproval**
- Follow platform advertising policies
- Use appropriate imagery and claims
- Ensure compliance with local regulations
- Appeal incorrect disapprovals
